## What Tempello Actually Does

Tempello is an AI-powered email billing automation tool built specifically for law firms. It connects to your email — Gmail via Google Workspace or Outlook via Microsoft 365 — monitors your inbox, and automatically identifies which emails relate to active client matters.

When it finds one, it matches the email to the right client and case (with 99% accuracy, per Tempello's published figures), writes a descriptive time entry, and logs it in your Clio or 8am MyCase account. The whole process happens in the background. You don't get an interruption. You don't fill out a form. You just... find more billable time waiting for you when you check the dashboard.

You get two operating modes:

- **Review Mode** — Tempello queues each entry for your approval before it posts. Good if you want to stay in control or you're just starting out.
- **Automatic Mode** — Entries post to Clio/MyCase without any action on your part. Good once you trust the system and want zero administrative overhead.

Either way, every matched email also gets saved directly to the relevant matter in your practice management system, so your case files stay complete and your billing records are detailed enough to hold up in court if that ever becomes relevant.

Setup is genuinely fast. You create a forwarding rule in your email system, connect your Clio or MyCase account through a secure API, and you're running. Most firms are up and capturing emails the same day they sign up.
